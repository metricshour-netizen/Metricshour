# MetricsHour - Project Overview

## Mission
Global financial intelligence platform connecting stock prices, country economics, bilateral trade data, and commodities in ONE fast mobile-first interface.

## What Makes Us Unique
Only platform that connects:
- Stock geographic revenue exposure (which countries each stock makes money from)
- Bilateral trade flows between countries (US-China trade deficit, etc)
- Country macroeconomic indicators (GDP, inflation, growth rates)
- Commodities data (oil, gold, metals affecting stocks)

Competitors have pieces. We have the complete picture.

## The Problem We Solve
**Before MetricsHour:** To understand "How does US-China trade tension affect my Apple stock?" requires:
- Yahoo Finance (stock price)
- Trading Economics (macro data)
- UN Comtrade (trade data)
- SEC EDGAR (geographic revenue)
- 30+ minutes manual research across 4 sites

**With MetricsHour:** One page, 30 seconds. All data connected.
